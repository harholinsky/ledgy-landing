FAQ

The eight questions everyone asks.

Is Ledgy free?

Yes — every solo-tracking feature is free forever. Cloud unlocks multi-device sync, sharing, AI voice/photo intake, Siri, Apple Watch logging, Telegram bot, Apple Wallet automation, and the web dashboard.

How much is Cloud?

$6.99/month or $59.99/year (~$5/mo). 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet. Ledgy is currently iOS-only via TestFlight beta. Android is on the roadmap — drop us a line if you want to be notified.

Does Ledgy work offline?

Yes. Every screen reads from a local SQLite database on your phone. New transactions appear instantly; sync happens in the background when you're back online.

What happens to my data if I leave a shared group?

Nothing. Your transactions are personally owned — sharing only decides who sees them. Leave the group, keep your history.

Is my voice sent to a server?

No. Voice is transcribed on your device using Apple's on-device speech recognizer. Only the resulting text is sent to our AI for parsing.

Can I import from Mint / YNAB / another app?

Yes. The CSV import wizard auto-detects column headers in 29 languages, currency symbols, and category aliases. Bring your full history.

What languages do you support?

14 fully translated locales: English, German, Spanish (Mexico), French, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese (Brazil), Russian, Turkish, Ukrainian, and Simplified Chinese.
